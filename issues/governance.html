Governance

Some Issues do not sort neatly into “here” or “abroad.”  They are questions of how we govern ourselves, and the policies under which, I think, government should conduct its operations.  I’ve put them here to seek your feedback, as on all Issues, and to solicit your ideas for additional ideas / concepts which haven’t occurred to me, yet.

Let Federal Workers Cut Red Tape!

Replacing a century-old, process-only bureaucratic model with the outcome-based Mission Command doctrine the military built after Vietnam — while keeping civil servants' jobs and pay secure by law.

Read More & Share Your Thoughts

AI:  Learning to Manage a Novel, Useful Tool

A federally coordinated research effort -- NIST, NASA, DARPA, and the NTSB, with industry standards bodies -- to characterize and make applicability recommendations for AI, the way military and vehicle-safety standards already work.

Read More & Share Your Thoughts

AI Disinformation:  Securing the Instruments of Truth

The real disinformation threat is not the fakes we can see — it is the corruption of the AI systems we rely on to tell real from fake.  Training data provenance requirements, adversarial red-teaming, and a public model-integrity registry.
