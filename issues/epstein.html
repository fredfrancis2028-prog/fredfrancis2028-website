Full Disclosure of Epstein Materials

The Problem

The American people deserve complete transparency regarding the Jeffrey Epstein case.  The persistent withholding and selective release of materials has eroded public trust and fueled justified suspicion that powerful individuals are being protected at the expense of justice and accountability. [1] [2]

This is not a partisan issue.  It is a test of whether our institutions serve the people or shield the powerful.  Every day that these materials remain hidden is a day that trust in our justice system erodes further.

Citations are preliminary.  Exact volume/page details will be verified in forthcoming white papers.

What I Propose

I demand the prompt and complete disclosure of all materials related to Jeffrey Epstein, without any second-guessing as to redundancy or any other qualification, as required by established law.  No exceptions.  No delays.  No redactions beyond what is strictly necessary to protect victims’ identities.

If there are individuals — regardless of party, position, or prominence — who are implicated by these materials, the public has an absolute right to know.  Justice delayed is justice denied, and the American people have been denied long enough.

The clear light of day is the light of freedom and liberty for us all.
